Recipes From Throughout My Life

Welcome! I made this page to share foods and recipes from my life.


BBQ Sauce and Ranch Sandwich

I know this sandwich sounds like quite the combo. I made this sandwich when I was about 7 years old, probably out of desperation. I remember them being good but I haven't eaten one of these in over 10 years. The sandwich represents how interesting I was as a child.

Ingredients:

  • Whole Wheat Bread
  • BQQ Sauce
  • Ranch

Egg and Tomato Dish

This dish is a favorite of mine from my mom's cooking. This is a Chinese dish which is also a bit of an unconventional combo but is very tasty. There is definitely a nostalgic factor to this dish and is a homey, comfort food that reminds me of childhood.

Ingredients:

  • Tomato
  • Egg
  • Salt
  • Vegetable Oil

More on Eggs

If eggs had a million fans I would be one of them. If eggs had 1000 fans I would be one of them. If eggs had 10 fans I would be one of them. If eggs had 1 fan it would be me. I have been on a consistent egg kick since I have been around 9 years old. When I was younger, I thought getting 2 yolks from one egg was the craziest thing ever. I thought getting two yolks was a sign of good luck and then started eating a lot of eggs to increase my chances. Unfortunately have not seen a double yolk in years :(


Breakfast Sandwiches

Why not talk more about eggs. I am also a big fan of breakfast sandwiches. I'm more of a savory breakfast girl rather than a sweet breakfast girl. While I'm sharing how I make my breakfast sandwich, the best breakfast sandwich is a Bacon Egg and Cheese (BEC) from the Needham Cafe Fresh. NON-NEGOTIABLE.

Ingredients:

  • Everything Bagel
  • Breakfast Sausage
  • Cheese
  • Egg
  • Optional: great with Frank's Hot Sauce

Overnight Oats

If I'm going to have a sweet breakfast, it's gonna be overnight oats. They are super easy to make and starts the day off right. I love to add fruit and granola to my oats, and it makes a super quick breakfast when I know I'll be in a rush.

Ingredients:

  • Rolled Oats
  • Oatmilk
  • Granola
  • Fruit
  • Syrup

Fried Rice

Fried rice is a comfort food that I would always be down to make. My mom taught me how to make fried rice and I love making it whenever I have left over rice now. Though fried rice is very inexpensive easy to make, I would be sooo excited whenever she would make it

Ingredients:

  • Leftover Rice
  • Cooking Oil
  • Green Onions
  • Soy Sauce
  • Veggies
  • Meat
