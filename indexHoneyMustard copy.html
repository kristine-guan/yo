Honey Mustard Reviews

Honey Mustard is my favorite condiment and here are my thoughts.


Ken's

Ken's is top tier store-bought honey mustard. This is my go-to honey mustard.


Sweet Baby Ray's

Honorable mention. Also a good store-bought honey mustard. Reminds me of a NH trip with friends when we took a chance on Baby Ray.


Heinz

Terrible. WPI needs to do better. I will NOT eat Heinz honey mustard.


Needham Town House Pizza

My favorite restaurant honey mustard (if they haven't changed it). I will try their honey mustard when I go back home. Pairs so well with Town House Pizza fries or chicken tenders.


Peter Christian's

A restaurant honey mustard that has a special place in my heart. From a restaurant in NH that I go to with my friends. If I remember correctly, this honey mustard is a bit darker in color than I prefer, but is pretty good! Not as sweet as Ken's.


Wild Rover

This review is just in from this weekend. Guest reviewers Lucy and Danny gave this honey mustard a twirl and Lucy said it might be her favorite yet! Maybe she got a bit ahead of herself...
